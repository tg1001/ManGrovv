Frequently Asked Question!

What is Eco-credit?

The ‘Eco-Credit’ means a singular unit of an incentive provided for a specified activity, delivering a positive impact on the environment. It's our own assesment system based on how eco-friendly your lifestyle is. These credits can be used to get discounts on products listed on our website and other offers.

How is my Eco-credit calculated?

It's calculated based on how Sustainable is your Building, energy usage, recycling rates, and other sustainable actions.

What can I do to improve my Eco-credit?

Actions such as using solar pannel, rain-water harvesting, planting trees, recycling, using eco-friendly products, etc can help you improve your Eco-credit.

What about business owners?

The service or products which are "Eco-mark" certified can be sold to our users.

How can I get my products listed?

Send us a mail about your product along with the "Eco-mark" certification.
